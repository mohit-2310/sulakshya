Why genuine spares matter

Compressor controllers log every operational malfunction precisely so periodic maintenance and repair are straightforward. Fitting the correct, genuine filters and oil protects the air-end — the heart of the machine — and keeps energy use and air quality where they should be. Look-alike parts often cost more in the long run through higher power bills and shorter component life.
